How To Play The Game of Life: College Edition


Setup

  • Type your name into the box & select the color of your choice for your game token.
  • Click 'Continue' to begin the game!



Object Of The Game

  • Travel the path of college as you move through new experiences!
  • Collect the most experience points to win the game.



Playing The Game

  • For each player's first turn, they will randomly be given a major.
  • For each turn, the player will first click the spinner and move that many spaces on the game board.
  • If you land on an end of semester space or new school year space, you will receive the amount of points listed for your major.
  • If you land on a yellow space, you will receive an event along with an increase in points or decrease in points. This will be added or subtracted from your point total.
  • After you spin, move, and complete the action on the space, your turn is over and it is the next player's turn.
  • Continue to play, move along the board, and collect points until you reach graduation!



How To Win

  • Once all players graduate and make it to the end of the board, the game is over.
  • The player with the most experience points wins!
